About Us

Who we are

Safehands Recognized social service organization.It provides helps to those in need in a multiple of ways.

At  SAFEHANDS, we are dedicated to making a profound difference in the lives of seniors and children. Our organization was founded by a group of passionate individuals who saw the need for a compassionate, supportive community that serves both old-age homes and child adoption services.

Safehands began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where every senior and every child feels valued, supported, and loved. Over the years, we have grown from a small team of dedicated volunteers to a comprehensive service provider, committed to enhancing the quality of life for seniors and facilitating the adoption process for children.

VISION

The mission of Safehands is to uphold Work for the transformation of lives of orphan and less privileged children, without discrimination in caste, creed or race&to rescue and rehabilitate them in our society. We want such children empowered through educational support so as to make them self-reliant and hopeful individuals in society.

MISSION

To uplift poor, Orphan, Homeless and less privileged children through social service and to help them lead healthy lives, by providing them with 24 by 7 medical aid and facilities. To provide shelter, care & Educational Support to them, empower them to rise above current circumstances, pursue their potential and become enhancing members of their society.
